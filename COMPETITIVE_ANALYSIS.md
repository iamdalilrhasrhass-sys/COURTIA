## 🎨 DESIGN PRINCIPLES (Phase 3+)

### Our Design Philosophy: "Beautiful Simplicity"

1. **Clarity First**
   - Every action has one obvious affordance
   - No hidden menus or mystery meat
   - Clear data hierarchy (bold, color, size)

2. **Minimal Cognitive Load**
   - Max 5 primary actions per screen
   - Consistent patterns across app
   - Smart defaults (auto-fill, suggestions)

3. **Delight in Details**
   - Smooth animations (not gratuitous)
   - Color psychology (trust = blue, alert = red)
   - Empty states are helpful, not sad
   - Error messages are friendly

4. **Mobile-First**
   - Thumb zone optimization
   - Gesture controls (swipe, long-press)
   - Touch targets >44px minimum
   - Offline-first sync

5. **Accessibility**
   - WCAG 2.1 AA compliance
   - Dark mode + high contrast
   - Screen reader support
   - Keyboard-only navigation
